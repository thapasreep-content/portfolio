01 / About

A writer who
follows the signal

I started in English Literature academia, moved into journalism, and then landed, happily, in the world of tech writing. What kept me there was the challenge: making complex things clear, making dry things interesting, and making technical things feel human.

Over 8+ years across India and Germany, I've worked on everything from RPA and IAM documentation to global recruitment reports with hundreds of thousands of readers, to cybersecurity marketing for a German SaaS startup. Each chapter added a layer: industry knowledge, a new content format, a new market.

These days I work at FlashX, a European IAM platform for MSPs, where I run content, social media, newsletter, and website communication across both English and German. I write for executives and for CTOs, for B2B buyers and for curious professionals.

I'm also studying Data & Discourse Studies at TU Darmstadt, which keeps me sharp on how language and data interact, a combination that's increasingly relevant in an AI-shaped world.
